About

Me

My name is Simone. I was born and raised in Southern Italy. I moved to Rome in 2005 and I still live there. I'm an User Interface developer. My skills covers many parts of the whole front end development process, so I do wireframes, analyze metrics, mock up ui elements in photoshop, build working prototypes, design and iterate through the product and develop its behavior based on a solid Javascript stack. I totally embrace the open source culture and I try to open as much as possible of what I make. Take a look at my github account.

What I don't do

There'are also a lot of things I don't do. Here they are:

  • I won't develop the front-end for your cms like Joomla, Magento, Wordpress, etc.
  • Make your site look the same on 32 different browsers on 671 different devices
  • Use that fancy jquery slideshow-parallax-multitouch-a-la-pinterest-plugin if the only reason for doing that is to be "cool"
  • By the way, when possible I try to avoid on relying on jquery plugins
