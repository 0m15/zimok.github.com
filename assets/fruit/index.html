fruit exhibition 2014

Bologna, PALAZZO RE ENZO – December, 14—16

Fruit Exhibition is an event centered on the best international graphic design and creative publications. Open to the public, Fruit Exhibition is the only event in Italy for paper and digital publishing including artists' books, catalogs, graphic design projects, periodicals, stationary and zines. In 2013 the fair featured 60 independent publishers and was attended by more than 5000 people.
